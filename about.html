About Gebeya Training

About Us

Gebeya Inc. is a Pan-African online Freelance Talent Marketplace headquartered in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. The company aims to become an active contributor to Africa’s digital transformation and build a booming Africa through leveraging continental and global job opportunities for African talent.Gebeya’s network of highly skilled, certified, and multilingual African talent on a digital platform services clients across the globe. Gebeya’s vision is of an Africa equipped with a competitive skilled workforce, and a greater number of innovative startups that will drive digital transformation.

Our mission is to be the most trusted and reliable source of talent that showcases Africa’s competitiveness. We commit to deliver exceptional value to our customers by offering skilled talent with the right mindset, attitude, and integrity.

Gebeya Program Educational Objectives

The educational objectives of the Gebeya Training Program are as follows:

  1. Graduates are qualified to be on Gebeya.com platform and be matched with clients who are looking for IT services. Once on this platform, Gebeya marketing team matches talents with clients all over Africa and the world.

  2. Graduates can be employed in industry, government, or entrepreneurial endeavors to demonstrate professional advancement through significant technical achievements and expanded leadership responsibility.

  3. Graduates can demonstrate the ability to work effectively as a team member and/or leader in an ever-changing professional environment.

  4. Graduates are able to advance and contribute to Gebeya's mission.
